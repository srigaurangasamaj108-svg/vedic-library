## HERO SECTION

Title:
Long-Term Vision

Subtitle:
Designing for Continuity Beyond a Generation

Intro Paragraph:

The Indian Knowledge Library is not conceived as a short-term initiative.

It is designed as a durable civilizational infrastructure —
built slowly, structured carefully,
and intended to remain stable across generations.

The goal is not rapid expansion.
The goal is structural permanence.

## SECTION 1 — TIME HORIZON

Heading:
A Multi-Generational Commitment

Content:

Most digital projects operate on product cycles.

Civilizational archives operate on time scales
measured in decades and centuries.

This project is designed for:

• 50-year architectural stability  
• 100-year canonical continuity  
• Intergenerational transmission  
• Scholarly durability  

Technologies may evolve.
Standards may shift.
Interfaces may change.

Canonical identity remains stable.

## SECTION 2 — STRUCTURED GROWTH MODEL

Heading:
Growth Without Structural Drift

Content:

Expansion is governed by:

• Phase discipline  
• Locking of completed layers  
• Explicit versioning  
• Spine declaration protocols  
• Schema enforcement  

Growth is additive, never destructive.

Earlier foundations are never rewritten.

Stability precedes expansion.
