# Vedic Corpus Map

This file defines the canonical structure of the Vedic Library corpus.

The folder structure under `/data` represents the full map of Vedic literature used by the system.

This structure must remain identical across:

- vedic-library-v1.0/data
- vedic-corpus-ingestion
- vedic-raw-data

---

# Root Corpus Divisions

## Śruti
Revealed scriptures of the Vedas.

- Rig Veda
- Yajur Veda
- Sama Veda
- Atharva Veda

Each contains:

- Samhita
- Brahmana
- Aranyaka
- Upanishad

---

## Smrti
Remembered scriptures including law, polity, and culture.

Includes:

- Dharma Sastra
- Artha Sastra
- Kama Sastra
- Niti Sastra

---

## Itihasa
Epic historical literature.

- Mahabharata
- Ramayana

---

## Purana

- Mahapurana (18)
- Upapurana

---

## Darshana
Philosophical systems.

- Nyaya
- Vaisesika
- Sankhya
- Yoga
- Mimamsa
- Vedanta

---

## Vedanga

Auxiliary sciences of the Veda.

- Siksa
- Chandas
- Vyakarana
- Nirukta
- Kalpa
- Jyotisa

---

## Upaveda

Applied sciences.

- Ayurveda
- Dhanurveda
- Gandharvaveda
- Sthapatyaveda

---

## Agama

Temple and ritual traditions.

- Saiva
- Sakta
- Vaisnava

---

## Sampradaya

Later theological traditions.

Currently includes:

- Gaudiya
